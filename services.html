The Ultimate Move-In Ready Checklist

We guarantee a "move-in ready" unit by focusing on every detail.

All Areas (Living, Beds, Halls)

  • All hard-surface floors vacuumed and mopped
  • All carpets vacuumed edge-to-edge
  • All baseboards, door frames, and windowsills hand-wiped
  • Ceiling fans, light fixtures, and light switches dusted and wiped
  • Closet shelves wiped down and floors vacuumed
  • Minor scuffs on walls and doors removed

Kitchen

  • Cabinets & drawers cleaned inside and out
  • Countertops & backsplash scrubbed and disinfected
  • Sink basin scrubbed, dried, and polished
  • Faucet polished
  • Exterior of all appliances (fridge, dishwasher, stove) degreased and shined
  • Range hood degreased
  • Microwave cleaned inside and out

Bathroom(s)

  • Tub & shower fully scrubbed (soap scum & hard water removed)
  • Shower glass cleaned streak-free
  • Tile & grout scrubbed
  • Toilet disinfected from top to bottom (base, tank, and bowl)
  • Vanity, sink, and fixtures cleaned and polished
  • Mirrors cleaned streak-free
  • Medicine cabinets cleaned inside and out
